## Common Questions

### Why does FrameShift need Accessibility permission?
Accessibility is required to move and resize windows when recalling a scene.

### Why does FrameShift request Screen Recording permission?
Screen Recording is used only to generate window thumbnails.
No video is recorded. No content is stored externally.

### Does FrameShift send any data to servers?
No. FrameShift operates entirely offline.
