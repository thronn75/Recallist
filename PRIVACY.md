# Privacy Policy

FrameShift is designed with privacy as a core principle.

## Data Collection

FrameShift does not collect, transmit, or store any personal data outside your device.

No analytics frameworks are used.
No tracking.
No third-party SDKs.

## Permissions

### Accessibility
Used exclusively to move and resize windows when recalling a saved scene.

### Screen Recording
Used only to generate static window thumbnails.
No video recording is stored.
No screen content is transmitted.

## Data Storage

All scene data, metadata, and thumbnails are stored locally within:

~/Library/Application Support/FrameShift/

Users may delete all stored data at any time by removing this directory.

## Changes

If this privacy policy changes, updates will be reflected in the repository.

---

FrameShift respects your workspace and your privacy.
